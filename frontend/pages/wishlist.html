Welcome to your personal Wishlist! Here, you can create, edit, and manage your wishes in a simple and efficient way. Whether it's for a special event, a gift, or a dream you've always had, this app will help you keep track of your goals.

What can you do in the Wishlist app?

  • Add new wishes for any event or occasion
  • Edit existing wishes to update details and status
  • Delete wishes you no longer need
  • Select multiple wishes for bulk actions like deletion
  • View the status of all your wishes and track progress
